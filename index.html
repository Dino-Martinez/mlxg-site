WHAT IS MLXG?

Multilux Gaming (MLXG) is an E-Sports organization dedicated to excellence.

As a newer organization that began its operation in Summer 2020, MLXG presents a unique opportunity for new players and brand partners to grow with us on our journey. Being a subsidiary of Multilux Media Group allows the organization to be agile and flexible when approaching new challenges.
